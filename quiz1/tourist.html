Monumen Nasional

Monumen Nasional (Monas) is a 132-meter obelisk in the center of Merdeka Square, Central Jakarta. It is the national monument of the Republic of Indonesia, built to commemorate the struggle for Indonesian independence. It is topped by a flame covered with gold foil. The monument and the museum are open daily from 08.00 until 16.00 Western Indonesia Time (UTC+7) throughout the week except for Mondays when the monument is closed.

Kota Tua

Kota Tua (Old Town) is a small area in Jakarta, Indonesia that spans across North Jakarta and West Jakarta. It has an area of 1.3 square kilometers and contains historical buildings from the colonial era of the Dutch East Indies. It was once known as “the Jewel of Asia” and “the Queen of the East” in the 16th century by European sailors. It offers various attractions and activities, such as museums, cafes, street food, and bicycle rental.

Photo by Visual Karsa on Unsplash

Taman Mini Indonesia Indah

Taman Mini Indonesia Indah (TMII) is a culture-based recreational area located in East Jakarta, Indonesia. It has an area of 140 hectares and showcases the diversity of Indonesian culture, flora, and fauna. It features various pavilions that represent the traditional houses, clothing, and artifacts of each province in Indonesia. It also has several museums, gardens, parks, and rides that offer educational and entertaining experiences for visitors.

Photo by Seorang Fadli on Unsplash

Ragunan

Ragunan Zoo is a zoo located in Pasar Minggu, South Jakarta, Indonesia. The zoo has an area of 140 hectares and houses more than 2,000 animals from various parts of Indonesia and the world. The zoo has an aviary and a primate center, and employs over 450 people. The zoo is open six days a week from 07:00 to 16:00 local time, except on Mondays when it is closed for animal holiday.

Photo by ALVENSIA ANGELA on Unsplash

Dufan

Ancol Dreamland (Dufan) is a theme park located in Ancol Bay City, North Jakarta, Indonesia. It is part of the Ancol Taman Impian complex, which is the largest integrated recreation park in Indonesia. Dufan offers various rides and attractions that are divided into nine zones: Indonesia, Jakarta, Asia, Europe, America, Greece, Hikayat, Kalila, and Fantasy Lights. Dufan also features live shows, events, and festivals throughout the year.

Photo on tiket.com

Tansera

Transera Waterpark is a water park located in Kota Harapan Indah, Bekasi, West Java, Indonesia. It is designed with an exotic African theme and provides two different zones: waterpark and drypark. The waterpark has various slides, pools, and attractions that are suitable for all ages. The drypark has a 4D simulator, a haunted house, a Ferris wheel, and other rides that offer fun and excitement.
